
# Literature dissection

## General Information

This repository contains a literature dissection exploring seasonal and
annual environmental conditions, relevant datasets, and peer-reviewed
articles related to shorebird and waterbird ecology at study sites in
California's Central Valley and the western Great Basin. After
completing this literature dissection, the following objectives were
addressed:

Describe seasonal or annual shifts in environmental conditions at a
sampling site relevant to the project Identify datasets or surveys used
in a poster/report/paper from the Cheadle Center Articulate the
connection between the study and other study systems or questions using
peer-reviewed articles Practice using citations in Quarto
